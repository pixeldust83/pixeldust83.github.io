About Us

Located in the heart of Downtown Brooklyn we are a graphic design duo hoping to make the world a prettier place one piece at a time.

Heidi

Graduate of the Art Insitute Chicago with a degree in Fine Arts. Her background includes an array of specialities including Interior Design, Fashion Merchandising, & Graphic Design specializing in Eccommerce optimization.

Tom

Graduate of the School of Visual Arts-Nyc with a degree in Illustration. He is currently working as a Graphic Designer for a print firm doing magazine publishing and constantly feeding his creative hunger doing comic book illustration.

Sebastian

Aka C-bass keeps our home office full of happiness and cuddles. He loves greeting guests at the door and finding the best Youtube cat videos.
